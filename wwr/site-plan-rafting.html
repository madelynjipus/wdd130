Overview

Purpose

The mission of Arat Na! Water Rafting Adventures is to promote adventure tourism, fostering outdoor education, and at the same time focusing on ecological conservation through rafting experiences.

Audience

The target audiences for Arat Na! Water Rafting Adventures are first, the adventure seekers, second, families, and lastly, corporate groups. Understanding their motivations and expectations will guide the site's content and design.

Scenario Question 1:
Scenario: How does an adventure enthusiast in his mid-30s, decide on a river rafting trip?
Answer: Being an adventure enthusiast, typically looks for challenging and adrenaline-pumping experiences. He considers factors such as the difficulty level of the rapids, the length of the trip, and the availability of other adventure activities nearby. He prefers companies that offer professional guides and safety equipment to ensure a thrilling yet safe experience.

Scenario Question 2:
Scenario: What factors influence a mother of young children, when planning a family-friendly river rafting trip?
Answer: Mothers prioritize safety and family-friendly activities. Mothers may look for rafting companies that offer calm and scenic river sections suitable for their children, with experienced guides that can cater to families. Mothers may also considers amenities such as changing facilities, picnic areas, and options for non-rafting activities like nature walks or wildlife viewing.

Scenario Question 3:
Scenario: How does a corporate group led by a team-building coordinator, choose a river rafting company for a team-building event?
Answer: The team-building coordinator and his/her corporate group may seek river rafting services that can accommodate large groups with customized packages that include team-building exercises on the river, such as rafting challenges or group activities like raft flipping drills. They may also check river rafting services that offer facilities for meetings or debriefing sessions after the rafting trip.
